Pizza

Description

Delicious and satisfying, our pizza is a perfect blend of crispy crust, flavorful tomato sauce, and gooey melted mozzarella cheese.
Topped with your favorite ingredients, it's a classic and customizable treat that promises a slice of comfort in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Recipe:

  1. Roll out the pizza dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Add your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 450°F (230°C) for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
