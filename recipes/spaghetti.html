Spaghetti

Description

Savor the simplicity of our spaghetti: al dente pasta embraced by a rich, savory tomato sauce, sprinkled with grated Parmesan.
This timeless dish offers a quick and comforting dining experience, perfect for any occasion.

Ingridents:

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Recipe:

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes to the pan and simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the flavorful tomato sauce until well coated.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil.
  6. Sprinkle generously with grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Serve and enjoy your quick and delicious spaghetti!
