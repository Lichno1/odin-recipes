Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of pasta
savory meat sauce, and creamy cheese, baked to perfection for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingridents

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 12 oz tomato paste
  • 2 tsp each of oregano and basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese mixed with 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Steps:

  1. Cook noodles; set aside
  2. Brown beef, onion and garlic; add tomatoes, paste, herbs, salt and pepper. Simmer.
  3. In a bow, mix ricotta and egg
  4. in a 9x13 inch dish, layer noodles, ricotta mix, meat sauce and cheese
  5. bake covered at 375Â°F for 25 minutes, then uncovered for 10-15 minutes
  6. Cool slightly, garnish and serve.
